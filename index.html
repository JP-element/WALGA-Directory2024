How to use the map

Zoom, pan, and tap any Local Government Area to reveal tailored details including population, infrastructure, and contact information.

  • Use the scroll wheel or pinch to zoom.
  • Click an LGA boundary to open its detailed profile.
  • Follow the website and email links for direct outreach.
